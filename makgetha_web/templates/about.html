Our Story

Premier Legal Counsel
in Potchefstroom

M. Makgetha Attorneys Inc. was founded with a clear vision: to deliver exceptional legal services that genuinely prioritise client needs and deliver tangible results. Our firm was built on the belief that every person — regardless of the complexity of their legal challenge — deserves access to experienced, principled and dedicated legal counsel.

Since our establishment in 2010, we have grown into one of Potchefstroom's most trusted law firms — relied upon by individuals, families and businesses across the North West Province for our integrity, depth of expertise and strategic approach to legal challenges. Our attorneys appear regularly in the Potchefstroom Magistrate's Court and the North West High Court, and we have built strong professional relationships throughout the South African legal system.

We understand that legal matters are often complex, deeply personal and consequential. Whether you are going through a painful divorce, defending your freedom in a criminal matter, pursuing compensation from the Road Accident Fund, or winding up a loved one's estate — you need attorneys who will treat your matter with the seriousness, skill and compassion it deserves. That is exactly what we offer.

We serve clients in Potchefstroom, Klerksdorp, Mahikeng, Rustenburg and throughout the North West Province, and we are available to provide advice to clients across South Africa on matters that do not require personal court attendance.

How We Work

Our Collaborative Approach

At M. Makgetha Attorneys, we follow a structured, client-centred process from the moment you first contact us through to the final resolution of your matter. We believe informed clients achieve better outcomes — which is why we prioritise clear communication, honest advice and transparent progress updates at every stage. Here is how we work:

01

Initial Assessment

We listen carefully to your situation, ask the right questions and analyse your matter from every angle to clearly identify the key legal issues and your options.

02

Deep Case Review

Thorough legal research, documentation review and evidence assessment to build a well-grounded, factually sound and legally robust foundation for your matter.

03

Strategy Development

A bespoke legal strategy tailored precisely to your objectives, your circumstances and the specific legal landscape of your matter — whether negotiation or litigation.

04

Active Representation

Assertive negotiation, skilled mediation or full courtroom litigation — executed with precision, persistence and a thorough command of South African law and procedure.

05

Resolution & Follow-up

We ensure every aspect of your matter is properly concluded, all orders are implemented, and we remain available to assist with any post-resolution questions or developments.

Our Principles

Values That Define
Our Practice

These are not just words on a wall — they are the principles that guide every decision we make, every client we serve, and every matter we handle at M. Makgetha Attorneys Inc.

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in every matter we handle. Our clients receive transparent, honest counsel — with no hidden agendas, no inflated billing and no false promises about outcomes. We tell you what you need to hear, not just what you want to hear.

Client Focus

Your goals, your circumstances and your wellbeing define our legal strategy. We take time to listen and truly understand your situation before advising, ensuring that every recommendation we make is genuinely in your best interest — not our own convenience.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest standards of legal practice — rigorous case preparation, thorough legal research and skilled courtroom representation. We never cut corners, and we never settle for good enough when better is achievable for our clients.

Discretion

Attorney-client privilege is fundamental and inviolable at our firm. Every consultation, every document and every detail of your matter is handled with the strictest confidentiality. Your personal, family and business affairs remain completely private — always.
