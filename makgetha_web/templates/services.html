Maintenance Matters

Ensuring adequate financial support for children and spouses is a fundamental legal right under South African law. Whether you are seeking maintenance, enforcing an existing order that is being ignored, or need to vary an order because circumstances have changed, our attorneys provide expert advice and vigorous representation at the Maintenance Court.

We understand how stressful it is when maintenance is withheld or insufficient. We act decisively to protect your rights and secure fair, legally enforceable maintenance orders without unnecessary delay.

What we assist with

  • Child maintenance claims and enforcement
  • Spousal and interim maintenance applications

Road Accident Fund Claims

If you or a family member has been injured in a road accident in South Africa, you may be entitled to significant compensation from the Road Accident Fund (RAF). The RAF compensates victims for medical expenses, loss of earnings, general damages and funeral costs — but navigating the claims process without legal representation is complex and can result in settlements far below what you are entitled to.

Our attorneys have extensive experience with RAF claims and know exactly how to document and present your claim for maximum compensation. We handle all correspondence with the RAF, brief medical experts where necessary, and represent you in court if the Fund disputes your claim or offers an inadequate settlement.

What we assist with

  • Road Accident Fund claims and lodgement
  • Personal injury and serious injury assessments
  • Loss of income and future earnings calculations
  • General damages for pain and suffering
  • Wrongful death and funeral expense claims

Unlawful Arrest Matters

Every person in South Africa has a constitutional right not to be arbitrarily arrested or detained. When the police or other authorities violate this right, you are entitled to hold them legally accountable. Our attorneys defend the constitutional rights of those who have been wrongfully arrested, unlawfully detained or subjected to police misconduct.

We act swiftly to challenge unlawful detention, pursue your release and, where appropriate, institute civil proceedings against the South African Police Service (SAPS) or other authorities for damages arising from the infringement of your rights.

What we assist with

  • Unlawful arrest and detention challenges
  • Bail applications and urgent release
  • Civil damages claims against SAPS
  • Malicious prosecution claims
  • Habeas corpus applications

Defending Your Constitutional Rights

Immigration Matters

South Africa's immigration system is complex, frequently changing and notoriously difficult to navigate without expert legal guidance. The Department of Home Affairs processes thousands of applications and appeals, and errors or omissions in your application can result in costly delays, refusals or even deportation. Our attorneys provide precise, up-to-date guidance on all immigration matters.

Whether you are a foreign national seeking to live and work in South Africa, a South African employer sponsoring a work visa, or a family seeking to reunite through a spousal or relative permit, we handle your matter with the expertise and attention to detail it demands.
