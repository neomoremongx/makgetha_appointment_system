Common Questions

Frequently Asked Legal Questions

We believe informed clients make better decisions. Here are honest answers to the questions we hear most often.

How much does an initial consultation cost?

We offer an initial consultation at a competitive rate so you can get professional legal advice before committing to a full matter. During this session, we assess your situation, explain your legal options and give you a clear picture of costs involved. Contact us to confirm current consultation fees.

How long does a divorce take in South Africa?

An uncontested divorce — where both parties agree on all terms including assets, maintenance and custody — can typically be finalised in 6 to 10 weeks. A contested divorce, where parties dispute one or more issues, can take several months to over a year depending on court availability and the complexity of the matter. Our attorneys work to resolve disputes efficiently and, where possible, through negotiation before litigation.

What should I do if I am arrested in Potchefstroom?

Remain calm and exercise your constitutional right to silence. Do not make any statement to the police without an attorney present. Contact M. Makgetha Attorneys Inc. as soon as possible — we can advise you, appear at your bail application and begin building your defence immediately. Prompt legal representation is critical in criminal matters.

Can I claim from the Road Accident Fund (RAF)?

If you were injured in a road accident in South Africa — whether as a driver, passenger, pedestrian or cyclist — you may be entitled to claim compensation from the Road Accident Fund for medical expenses, loss of income and general damages. Strict prescription periods apply, so it is important to act quickly. Our attorneys handle RAF claims on a contingency basis in appropriate cases.

How do I report and wind up a deceased estate?

When a person passes away, their estate must be reported to the Master of the High Court within 14 days. The process then involves appointing an executor, liquidating and distributing assets, settling debts and filing the necessary accounts. This can take several months. Our attorneys manage the entire process, reducing the administrative burden on grieving families.

Do you assist with immigration matters?

Yes. We assist with a wide range of immigration applications including temporary and permanent residence permits, critical skills work visas, corporate visas, spousal and partner visas, and appeals against decisions by the Department of Home Affairs. Immigration law is complex and subject to frequent regulatory changes — professional guidance significantly improves your chances of success.
